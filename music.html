Music has played a major role in my life ever since I bought my first guitar at age 13. It is the one thing that consistently grounds me when life's stresses carry me away. Over time, my pursuit has led me to the bass guitar, drums, and music production software. Using the DAW (digital audio workstations) Ableton, Ive produced hundreds of my own tracks under the alias, 'Lord Dinosaur'. Some of my productions include contributions for the soundtrack to the competative gaming documentary "The Smash Brothers".
