After graduating high school from Scranton Preparatory School, I went on to attain my Bachelor's Degree in Communication and Media Studies from The University of Scranton. Upon entry into the real world, I arrived at the local PBS affiliate, WVIA. I quickly moved from various roles as Production Assistant to having complete creative control as Director, Videographer & Editor over a variety of programs fit for television broadcast and digital distribution. Types of productions included:

  • 60 minute documentaries - historical/biographical
  • 30 minute programs - arts & performance/educational
  • Digital shorts
  • Reality TV
  • Trailers

Following my experiences at WVIA, I spent a few years as a News Photographer/Editor at the local ABC afiliate, WNEP. It is here where I truly experienced the value of time mangagement and teamwork, collaborating on stories under pressing deadlines that impacted my local community.
